Voice Phishing Awareness

What is Vishing?

Voice phishing or vishing is a form of social engineering attack, where fraudsters make phone calls to deceive individuals into disclosing sensitive data, including passwords, bank accounts, or other personal information. Such attacks are very convincing and dangerous.

Human Factor

Social engineering attacks exploit human behavior like trust, fear, urgency, and ignorance. Many victims fall prey due to psychological manipulation and not technical means.

Awareness & Education

Cybersecurity awareness is key. Informed users can identify red flags and avoid sharing sensitive information. Digital education and awareness programs help reduce phishing attacks.

AI Protection

Advanced AI systems analyze calls, voice patterns, and conversations in real-time to detect suspicious behavior. Machine learning models help warn users before damage occurs.

Project Context

This project is a multimedia web app for SmartCall AI - Vishing Awareness Campaign, using images, videos, audio, and 3D elements for an interactive learning experience.
